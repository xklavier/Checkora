### Productivity, Redefined.

Checkora is a minimalist task management companion and frictionless checklist tool. Currently in **TestFlight**, it is designed to strip away the noise of traditional "to-do" apps, focusing on the core essentials of getting things done with speed and elegance.

[**Join the TestFlight**](https://github.com/xklavier/Checkora)

---

### Core Philosophy

- **Frictionless Entry** — Capture tasks as fast as you think of them. No complex forms, just pure productivity.
- **Minimalist Interface** — A refined design that emphasizes your current focus, helping you manage your daily grind without being overwhelmed.
- **Intentional Checklists** — Perfect for recurring routines, grocery lists, or project milestones, designed to be as simple as pen and paper but more powerful.
- **Sleek Interactions** — Satisfying haptics and animations that make checking off a task feel like a win.
